# Hospitality Pulse AI

Welcome to Hospitality Pulse AI, an intelligent dashboard designed to transform raw guest feedback into actionable insights. This application leverages the power of generative AI to analyze customer reviews, providing a comprehensive overview of guest sentiment, key concerns, and actionable suggestions for improvement.

## Key Features

*   **AI-Powered Review Analysis**: Upload your customer reviews in a `.csv` or `.xlsx` file, and the application will automatically analyze each review for sentiment (Best, Good, Fare, Bad) and categorize it by topic (e.g., Food, Management Service, Reservation).
*   **Interactive Dashboard**: Visualize your data through a series of interactive charts and cards:
    *   **Sentiment Snapshot**: Get a high-level view of overall guest satisfaction and a summary of feedback.
    *   **Sentiment Distribution**: See the breakdown of positive vs. negative feedback at a glance.
    *   **Actionable Suggestions**: Receive AI-generated recommendations grouped by topic to help you prioritize improvements.
    *   **Key Positives**: Quickly identify what your guests love most.
    *   **Department-Level Insights**: Analyze feedback distribution and negative sentiment by department.
    *   **Sentiment Trends**: Track guest satisfaction over time with a monthly average rating chart.
    *   **Detailed Review List**: Browse, filter, and sort through individual reviews and even generate AI-powered replies.
*   **Batch Processing**: The application is built to handle large volumes of reviews by processing them in manageable batches to ensure stability and performance.
*   **Modern Tech Stack**: Built with Next.js, React, ShadCN, Tailwind CSS, and Genkit for powerful AI capabilities.

## How to Get Started

1.  Run the application.
2.  On the main screen, either drag and drop your reviews file (in `.xlsx` or `.csv` format) into the upload area or click **Browse Files** to select it.
3.  Alternatively, click **Test with Mock Data** to see the dashboard in action with a pre-loaded set of sample reviews.
4.  The AI will process the reviews, and the dashboard will populate with detailed analytics and insights.
